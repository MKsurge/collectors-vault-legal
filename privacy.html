Privacy Policy

Collectors Vault · Effective date: 10 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dreamline Studio Ltd (“Dreamline Studio”, “we”, “us” or “our”), the owner and operator of the Collectors Vault mobile application (the “App”), collects, uses, shares and protects your personal data when you use the App, and the rights you have over that data.

By creating an account or using the App you acknowledge this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with it, please do not use the App.

1. Information we collect

CategoryDetailsSource
Account information Email address and a password (stored only in securely hashed form — we never see or store your plain-text password). Provided by you at sign-up.
Coin photos Photos of coins that you take with the camera or choose from your photo library for identification, and copies you choose to save to your collection. Provided by you when you use the scan feature.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, browsing history or advertising identifiers. The App contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and we do not track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the service — identifying coins from your photos, storing your collection, showing your dashboard, and operating your account.
  • AI coin analysis — when you scan a coin, the photo is sent securely to our backend, which forwards it to our AI provider (Anthropic) to identify the coin and estimate its value. Results are returned to you and stored only if you choose to save them.
  • Subscription management — validating purchases with Apple/Google and unlocking Premium features.
  • Service protection — enforcing scan limits, preventing fraud and abuse, and securing accounts.
  • Legal compliance — meeting our legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Use.

3. Legal bases (UK / EEA users)

Where UK GDPR or EU GDPR applies, we process your data on these bases: performance of a contract (providing the App and your subscription), legitimate interests (securing and improving the service, preventing abuse), and consent (access to your camera and photo library, which you may withdraw at any time in your device settings).

4. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with advertisers. We share data only with the service providers needed to run the App:

5. International transfers

Your data is stored in the United Kingdom (London) by our hosting provider. Coin photos sent for AI analysis are processed by Anthropic in the United States. Where personal data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement or Standard Contractual Clauses implemented by our providers.

6. How long we keep your data

We keep your data for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (see section 7), your coin records, photos, usage counters and subscription records are permanently deleted from our systems, and your authentication record is removed. Backups held by our infrastructure provider expire on a rolling basis shortly afterwards.

7. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the App:

  1. Open the App and sign in.
  2. Go to the Account tab.
  3. Under Danger zone, tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and irreversible: it removes your account, your entire coin collection, all photos and usage records. If you cannot access the App, you can request deletion by emailing hussein.abdullah.hussein1949@gmail.com from your registered email address; we will complete the request within 30 days.

Note: deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription. Cancel your subscription in your Apple or Google subscription settings, otherwise the store may continue to bill you.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; correct inaccurate data; delete your data; restrict or object to processing; data portability; and withdraw consent. UK/EEA users may lodge a complaint with their supervisory authority (in the UK, the Information Commissioner’s Office, ico.org.uk). California residents have equivalent rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know, delete, and correct — and we do not sell or “share” personal information as defined by that law.

To exercise any right, use the in-app deletion feature or contact us at the email address below.

9. Security

We protect your data with industry-standard measures: encrypted connections (TLS) for all traffic, encrypted storage, hashed passwords, private photo storage accessible only through short-lived signed links, and row-level security rules that restrict every database record to the account that owns it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data appropriately.

10. Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be signposted in the App or by email. The “Effective date” above always shows when this policy was last revised. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
